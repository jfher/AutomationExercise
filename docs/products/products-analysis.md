# Products Module Analysis

## Module

Products

---

## Objective

Allow users to browse, search and inspect products before purchasing.

---

## Functional Requirements

### FR-PROD-001

Users can view all available products.

---

### FR-PROD-002

Users can search products by keyword.

---

### FR-PROD-003

Users can open the product detail page.

---

### FR-PROD-004

Users can filter products by category.

---

### FR-PROD-005

Users can filter products by brand.
