# Cart Module Analysis

## Module

Cart

---

## Objective

Allow users to manage products before checkout.

---

## Functional Requirements

### FR-CART-001

Users can add products to the shopping cart.

---

### FR-CART-002

Users can view products in the cart.

---

### FR-CART-003

Users can remove products from the cart.

---

### FR-CART-004

Users can update product quantity.

---

### FR-CART-005

Users can continue shopping.

---

### FR-CART-006

Users can proceed to checkout.
